About us

Hello dear visitors,
My name is Abdulrahman Folly, and I would like to share with you the story of my creativity and my new website that I developed with passion and enthusiasm. At the age of seventeen, I realized that my strong passion for programming and web design and development could turn into a unique project that caters to the interests of cinema lovers, and thus my movie rating website was born.

I built this website to be a comprehensive source of information about movies and to give users the opportunity to rate the movies they watch. You can create a personal account and record your own ratings, as well as browse through other users' ratings and discover new movies that you may not have been aware of before.

I wanted the design to be appealing and the interface to be user-friendly, so I developed it with my skills in web design and development. You will find that searching for movies and browsing through them is easy and convenient, as you can search based on genre, year, actors, and more. I am very satisfied with the final result, and I hope that visitors will enjoy the browsing experience on the website.

I am extremely proud to have developed this website, and certainly, such an accomplishment would not have been possible without the support of my family and friends who inspired me and encouraged me to continue. I believe that technology has the power to change the world and improve our lives, and I strive to be part of that transformation.

I hope that users find value in my website and enjoy the experience of movie rating and discovering new cinematic works. I will always work on improving the website and providing exciting and useful content for every visitor.

Thank you very much for your support and trust, and I look forward to seeing your comments and experiences on our website.

Best regards, Abdulrahman Folly
